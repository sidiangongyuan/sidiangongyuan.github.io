Master's research portfolio, 2023-2026

Kang Yang

MS Student, School of Information, Renmin University of China

My master's research studies how connected vehicles and infrastructure share compact, useful representations for robust 3D perception: from camera-based BEV depth refinement, to anchor-centric communication, to query and instance messages, to uncertainty-aware and loss-resilient collaboration, to online adaptation for open-world heterogeneity.

Collaborative perception research arc

The work moves from improving camera-based BEV collaboration, to replacing dense feature-map communication with sparse anchors and queries, to efficient multimodal instance communication, to robustness under uncertainty and packet loss, and finally to online adaptation when unfamiliar agents meet at deployment.
